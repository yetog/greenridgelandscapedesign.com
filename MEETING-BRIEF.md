## What Was Built

A fully custom, SEO-optimized static website for Green Empire Landscaping:

- **41 pages** — homepage, 7 service pages, 20 city/area pages, about, FAQ, reviews, blog articles, legal
- **Config-driven generator** — all content lives in one file (`site.config.json`). Change anything, regenerate in 1 second.
- **Local SEO structure** — every city page targets `"landscaping [city] NY"` with full LocalBusiness schema
- **7 services** — repositioned around outdoor renovation (patios, pavers, driveways, outdoor living, landscape design)
- **Lead capture** — estimate form on every page, SMS opt-in, 1-hour response promise
- **Real photos** — 8 AI-generated project photos placed across service cards, gallery, and hero

**On the SEO strategy:**
> "The franchise sites — Grounds Guys, TruGreen — they have one location page per city. We have a full page per city with local copy, local schema, and the exact search terms your customers type. That's the edge."

## The Ask — How to Close

Don't oversell. End the demo with:

> "The site is built and live. The next step is two things: get the form wired so leads come in, and decide how you want to handle the response — manual or automated. That's where the real ROI is. Want to talk through that now?"

That pivots naturally into the automation conversation without overselling a product that isn't finished.
